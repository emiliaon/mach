Wait, Assembly?

Compilers are great, but for the hottest paths in performance benchmarking, predictability is everything. Mach uses hand-written SIMD instructions to ensure every cycle counts.

  • ARM64: NEON fadd, fmin, fmax vectorization.
  • x86_64: SSE2 addpd, minpd, maxpd optimization.
